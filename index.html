Our Implemented Solutions

Smart homes offer constant monitoring and controlling . Smart homes can detect the residents behaviors and activities through sensors and actuators installed throughout the unit.A system has been developed to control the internal and external lighting, temperature systems and fire alarm of the house by using LabVIEW.
The solution is a multi-node (i.e. multi-site) remote monitoring system that utilizes an NI cRIO-based controller with customized NI InsightCM monitoring software.The remote monitoring system monitors equipment condition by taking several vibration signal measurements along with reading over 500 Modbus registers.
